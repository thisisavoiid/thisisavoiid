# 👤 About Me

### Hello, I’m Jonathan 👋

I am a **Games Programming student at SAE Institute Bochum**, specializing in building functional, clean, and highly maintainable digital experiences. My technical focus revolves around **C# and Python**, where I prioritize modular architecture, systematic problem-solving, and robust implementation across diverse software domains.

---

### 🚀 Core Specializations

* **Systematic Software Design**
  Developing **modular, highly configurable software solutions** and utility applications. Strong emphasis on **type safety** (structs/enums) and a **clear separation of concerns** using manager-based architectures.

* **Game & Engine Systems**
  Implementing core **game mechanics, custom I/O systems, and foundational data structures** (such as specialized grid and coordinate management) for interactive applications.

* **Infrastructure & Automation**
  Designing and maintaining local environments to streamline **development workflows, automated testing, and scripting**.

* **Creative Tech & Audio Logic**
  Applying precise programmatic logic to creative domains, including **custom audio synthesis** and **optimized console visualization**.

> **🧠 Analytical & detail-oriented.**
> **💻 Committed to clean, production-ready code.**
> **🎧 Driven by innovation at the intersection of logic and creativity.**
